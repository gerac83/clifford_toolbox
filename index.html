Introduction

Clifford Toolbox is a collection of functions for doing general calculations with Clifford Algebra (i.e. Geometric Algebra) of , using Mathematica 9.0 or higher and Matlab R2013a or higher. All results are given in terms of the orthonormal basis vectors .

If you use this software please cite the following paper:

G. Aragon-Camarasa, G. Aragon-Gonzalez, J. L. Aragon and M. A. Rodriguez-Andrade, Clifford Algebra with Mathematica. ArXiv e-prints, October 2008. URL: http://arxiv.org/abs/0810.2412<\b>
